Card Structures

Ellabolarates card's content structure

  1. Card Generic
    1. Image
    2. Title
    3. Excerpts
    4. Date & Time
    5. CTA
  2. Card - Job Channel (paid)
    1. Job channel logo
    2. Job Channel Title
    3. Ad status (published / moderated / disabled)
    4. Price
    5. CTA
  3. Card - Job Channel (free)
    1. Job channel logo
    2. Job Channel Title
    3. Ad status (published / moderated / disabled)
    4. Toggle
  4. Card Hiring stage
    1. Header
      1. Job Title
      2. Job Information
      3. Job department
      4. Job location
      5. Job code
      6. Configuration / setting / option
    2. Body
      1. Job Stage
      2. Number of applicant / candidate
      3. Stage name
      4. Carousel navigation
    3. Footer
      1. Job information status
      2. Link to advertise job
      3. Total applicants / candidates
      4. Job creation date
