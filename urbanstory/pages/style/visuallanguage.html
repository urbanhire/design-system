Visual Language

What is Visual Language?

Visual language helps us as a brand to communicate our brand through visualisation of ideas.
These visual elements tie together a group in a structured and conventional way that people understand.
This could be in form of illustrations, icons, colours, style guide, etc.

We believed in...

Opportunity

We believe in equal opportunity for everyone

Universality

We're easy to understand

Transparency

We believe in data transparency

Modern

We're tech-savvy

Illustrations Guidelines

Use border, on borders

Make seamless illustrations, and don't use borders on them unless they are borders or for framing purposes.

Monochrome

For corporate considerations, we believe it is more professional to use monochrome colours on blue shades.

Dynamic

Project activities within the illustrations as much as possible. It projects us as the agile-startups.

Colorful on Focal

If ever needed can use colours others than our color pallete to make the focal point stands out.
